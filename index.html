How to use the Metaphor Builder

This tool generates personalised therapeutic metaphors drawn from your client's own world — their hobbies, job, language and life experience. The more specific your input, the more targeted the output.

1 Quick Mode — designed for use during a session. Fill in three fields and get one ready-to-use metaphor in seconds.
2 Full Mode — designed for pre or post-session preparation. Add more detail for richer, more structurally targeted output.
3 Conversational version — use this naturally in the session before hypnosis. It works by slipping past the conscious mind and planting the idea at a deeper level, so that when the hypnotic version is delivered during trance, it reinforces what has already begun to take root.
4 Hypnotic version — designed to be read aloud during trance, deepening and reinforcing the metaphor introduced in conversation.
5 These are guides, not scripts. Adapt the language freely to suit your client, your therapeutic style, and the moment. The metaphors are a starting point — your clinical judgment shapes how they are used.
