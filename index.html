How to use the Metaphor Builder

Generate personalised therapeutic metaphors drawn from your client's own world. The more specific your input, the more targeted the output.

1Quick Mode — for use during a session. Three fields, one metaphor, ready in seconds.
2Full Mode — for pre or post-session preparation. More detail, richer output.
3Conversational version — use naturally in session before hypnosis to plant the idea at a deeper level.
4Hypnotic version — read aloud during trance to reinforce what's already taken root.
5These are guides, not scripts. Adapt freely to suit your client and your style.
